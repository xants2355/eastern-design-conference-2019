Studio Marvil + TON / CZ

linkedin



Studio Marvil belongs to the best in Czech design since its establishment in 1995. Their work brings them into contact with creatives from many fields, including architects, designers, illustrators, photographers, film-makers and authors. They have won series of prizes and they’re the first graphic design studio in the Czech Republic to receive a prestigious Red Dot Design Award which they won again this year.

TON (the abbreviation of - Továrna Ohýbaného Nábytku – bent furniture factory) is a Czech furniture company based in Bystrice pod Hostynem. They specialise in unique wood-bending technology which they use since 1861.

Studio Marvil and TON come to Eastern to talk about their cooperation that brings the iconic design product from the 19th century to the business spotlight of the 21st century.

Liv Siddall is one half of Mare Street Market on Air and is also Features Editor of smart women’s magazine Riposte. Alongside this she is a writer, editor and interviewer predominantly focused on the world of music, arts and culture and the independent magazine scene. Formerly the Features Editor at It’s Nice That and the Founder and Editor of Rough Trade Magazine, her love for radio has punctuated her career so far seeing her host the weekly It’s Nice That podcast and oversee the launch of Rough Trade Radio.

If not traveling, Lars Harmsen lives and works in Munich where he is partner and creative director at MELVILLE Brand Design. He launched the fontlabel Volcano-Type and is the founder of SLANTED PUBLISHERS blog and magazine. He also is professor of typography and design at the University of Applied Sciences and Arts Dortmund. Erik Sikora / Sk

instagram


His dream as an artist is to develop a new format of post-lecture. The post-lectures concern defence of one’s own life. From the content perspective, it is a combination of teaching skills with the tendencies to save the world or eventually the tendency to make a bundle during saving the world. If Džumělec does not make a bundle with his own ideas, he upcycles his ideas in the songs that work as kind of fillers to slightly embarrassing breaks. A secret reason for his post-lectures in the public area is his aspiration to get more likes to grow the social media bubble.
‍
Erik comes to Eastern to explain & perform the unorthodox approach to create visual & non-visual content that will instantly make your day.

Ali Ali / EG

instagram


Ali Ali is an acclaimed advertising director director from Cairo, Egypt and the Founder of Good People. In a remarkably short period, he became the region's most sought-after director, having recieved numerous international awards for his work. In addition to a Black Cube, a D&AD yellow pencil, and six Cannes Lions, Ali has picked up the TV Grand Prix five times at Dubai Lynx. With over ninety million YouTube views, his “Never Say No to Panda” was named one of the most watched tv spots of all time by the Guardian UK. Born in Egypt and raised all over the world, Ali's work shows an attentiveness to local realities as well as an international sensibility.
‍
Ali comes to Eastern to talk about the local and international in him that allowed for his cult advertising campaigns to rock the advertising world while staying true and funny as hell.

Petra Dočekalová / CZ

twitter
instagram


Petra Dočekalová studied at the Type Design and Typography studio at Academy of Arts, Architecture and Design in Prague. Since 2013 she is a member of Suitcase & Briefcase Type Foundry team, working on all activities associated with foundries. She is focused on editorial work such as book Scripts for shop windows and Typo 9010, that won several awards all over the world. She is also a type designer, sign painter and lettering artist, working on new digital scripts.

She received TDC Award of excellence for her diploma project dealing with the Czechoslovak calligraphy and new hand lettering forms.

Petra comes to Eastern to talk about resurrecting the craft of sign painting that has disappeared over the years with the advance of printing technologies in the Czech Republic.

Oliver Klimpel / DE



Oliver works in a hybrid practice at the intersection of design, art and research. After many years in London, he’s now based in Berlin currently designing the new foyer interior of Austrian museum Kunsthaus Graz - including exhibition displays and shop facilities. His work for the exhibition “Creative Infidelities” at arc en réve – centre d’architecture Bordeaux, France, is now on show at the Museum of Contemporary Art in Leipzig. He was Professor at the Leipzig Academy of Visual Arts from 2008 til 2015; other educational projects have been an important part of his practice, having been himself trained as a graphic designer at the Leipzig Academy of Visual Arts and the Royal College of Art London. In 2016 he was the moderator of the International Biennial of Graphic Design Brno.

Oliver comes to Eastern to guide you through the conference as a host and moderator.

Tabačka Kulturfabrik

Tabačka is a unique center and open area for art, creativity and cooperation in Košice, Slovakia. Grounds of the former tobacco factory on Gorky Street offer 2500 m2 of contemporary art, concerts, theatre productions, performances, movies, education, cooperation, trendy bistro and much more.

Košice

The decision to hold Eastern in Košice, Slovakia does not stem from the fact the 3 of us (founders and organisers) have been active in the city for many years. It is the potential of the city and the constant movement in it that made us think of it as a good spot in the time when Košice is becoming a more-exciting, less-regulated, more-eastern type of cultural metropolis full of thriving grass-roots initiatives. Roman calls it the Slovak Berlin.

About

Eastern Design Conference brings together design and business. Definition of ‘East’ is best represented through the optics of geographical and market-related outskirts. Geographically it’s Central and Eastern Europe. With design being a world-wide phenomenon, the market-related outskirts are defined by our local eastern ways of doing business in a post-communist business environment. Eastern was founded to explore and define this relationship.


WHY

We strive to showcase the unusual yet effective design thinking native to the East.

E
HOW

By creating a place to exchange knowledge about eastern design and business environment and our experience.

A
WHAT

Taking a closer look on the differences between the Eastern and the Western business environment in relation to design.

S
WHERE

Eastern is a two day long design conference taking place in Košice, Slovakia on 26–27th October. It features forward thinking designers from the unexplored outskirts of Eastern Europe.
